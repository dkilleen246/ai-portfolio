# Case Studies

End-to-end write-ups of real AI workflow problems — showing the full arc from problem definition through iteration to a working, repeatable solution.

## Structure

Each case study includes:
- **Problem** — what needed solving and why it mattered
- **Approach** — the workflow or strategy chosen
- **Tools Used** — which AI models and supporting tools were involved
- **Process** — step-by-step breakdown with examples
- **Result** — what the final output looked like
- **Lessons Learned** — what worked, what didn't, and what to reuse
